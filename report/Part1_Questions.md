# Part 1: Basic SQL Questions

## 1.1 Product Catalog
List all products in the Electronics category. Show ProductID, ProductName, and
UnitPrice. Order by price from highest to lowest.

---

## 1.2 Customer Count
How many customers are in each region? Show Region and the count of customers.

---

## 1.3 Recent Orders
Show the 10 most recent orders. Display OrderID, OrderDate, and TotalSales.

---

## 1.4 Affordable Products
Find all products with a UnitPrice less than R1000. Show ProductName, Category, and
UnitPrice.

---

## 1.5 Customer Satisfaction
Count how many feedback responses exist for each Satisfaction level. Order by count
descending.
